Vincent van Gogh was one of the world’s greatest artists, with paintings such as ‘Starry Night’ and ‘Sunflowers,’
though he was unknown until after his death.

"One may have a blazing hearth in one’s soul and yet no one ever came to sit by it. Passers-by see only a wisp of smoke from the chimney and continue on their way." - Vincent Van Gogh

BRIEF OVERVIEW

  • The Father of Modern Art
  • Mentally ill: bipolar affective disorder
  • Was bullied for a lifetime even by kids
  • Terrible poverty and loneliness
  • Had only one painting sold

As he lay on his death bed, having apparently shot himself, Vincent Van Gogh is said to have uttered the final words, "The sadness will last forever".

WIDELY KNOWN

  • More than 2,100 works
  • Had his ear cut
  • Committed suicide
